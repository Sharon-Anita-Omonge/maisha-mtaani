About Us

History

Founded in 2015, Maisha Mtaani emerged as a response to the pressing drug abuse epidemic in Kisumu County. Our journey began with a passionate commitment to uplift our community, providing youth and vulnerable individuals with hope, opportunity, and a brighter future.

Mission

Empower and uplift youth and vulnerable populations in Kisumu County through holistic support, positive change, and opportunities for growth.

Vision

Create a community where everyone has access to healthcare, education, and employment, while eradicating drug abuse and fostering a vibrant, hopeful society.

Pillars

  • Healthcare & Mental Wellness
  • Agriculture
  • Energy & Technology
  • Sports & Entertainment
  • Leadership

Core Values

  • Holistic development
  • Empowerment
  • Inclusivity
  • Sustainability
  • Transparency & accountability

Goals

At Maisha Mtaani, our goals are clear and ambitious. We strive to empower youth and vulnerable populations through socio-economic initiatives that break the cycle of drug abuse. We aim to cultivate holistic growth, offering quality healthcare, education, and employment opportunities to all. Our vision is a transformed community where every individual thrives.

Impact

Over the years, Maisha Mtaani has made significant strides. Our initiatives have successfully rehabilitated addicts, offering them meaningful employment and a fresh start. Through under-16 football tournaments, prison visits, mentorship sessions, and more, we've worked to deter drug abuse and inspire positive change. We're also committed to environmental sustainability, fostering initiatives like tree planting, renewable energy solutions, and plastic cabros.
