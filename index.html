Welcome To
Maisha Mtaani,

a dedicated community-based organization committed to uplifting the youth and vulnerable populations of Kisumu County, Kenya. Our journey began in 2015 as a response to the escalating drug abuse crisis that was deeply affecting our community. Our belief is grounded in the fundamental principle that every individual should have access to quality healthcare, education, and meaningful employment opportunities. This belief fuels our unwavering dedication to realizing this vision within our community.
