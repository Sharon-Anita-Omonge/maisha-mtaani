Maisha Mtaani Events

Creating awareness about drug abuse is a crucial initiative to address a pressing issue in many communities. Here are a series of events that our organization, Maisha Mtaani, organize to raise awareness about drug abuse:

2023 Events So Far

1. Community Awareness Day - 5th August

Join us for an engaging day filled with activities, dialogue sessions, and inspiring talks as we raise awareness about drug abuse and its consequences.

2. Empowerment Workshops

Our regular workshops empower youth with skills and knowledge, providing a platform for growth and self-improvement.

3. Soccer Tournament

Our under-16 soccer tournament promotes teamwork, sportsmanship, and a healthy lifestyle among the youth in our community.
